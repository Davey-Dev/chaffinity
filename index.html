HOW TO GET MORE FPS ON SCORESPY

Made by Davey Dollar. Inspired by the CloneHeroBooster tool made by GHFear.

This method is essentially doing exactly what CloneHeroBooster does but just doing it manually.

Insert the number of Logical Processors you have below. You can find this value on the "Performance" screen of Task Manager.





Once you input your logical processors above and press submit, you'll want to follow these instructions below.
  1. Open Clone Hero or ScoreSpy
  2. Open Task Manager and find "Clone Hero.exe" in the processes tab
  3. Right click on it and press "Go to Details"
  4. Right click on it again in this menu and click on "Set affinity"
  5. Untick "All Processors" and then tick the logical processors listed in the box above.
Your results may vary depending on how many logical processors you have.
You will need to set these processors every time you start the game.
